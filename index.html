Overview of the Trumpet

Page 2 Page 3 Page 4

The trumpet is one of the most notorius instruments in the world. It's unique sound has made it easily recognizable in a band and one of the most popular instruments for beginner musicians.

The earliest metal trumpets are thought to have been made around 1500 BC. Trumpets made of bronze and silver were found in King Tut's grave. This, however, is not necessarily the origin of people creating sound by blowing through something. There are records of people in early civilizations blowing through conch shells or other shells to create sound.

Throughout its development, there have been many iteratiions of the trumpet. From earliest to latest they are:

  1. Graves Post Horn (1845)
  2. Husson et Buthod (1850)
  3. Cornet or Trumpet in Bb and G by E.G. Wright (1860)
  4. Gautrot Trumpets (1867)
  5. Courtois Trumpets (1885)
  6. Boston Trumpets (1887)
  7. Courtois Trumpets (1897)
  8. Conn Trumpets (1899)
  9. Besson Trumpets aka The Modern Trumpet (1910)
